90 Day Residential Program

Our 90 Residential Program offers a structured atmosphere for long term clients whose goal is to continue into one of our sober living programs.
A little longer stay helps to ensure a stronger foundation prior to entering sober living.

Sober Living

After completion treatment we offer sober living. This is a good transition step for clients that might be ready to get to living, but still might benefit from structured living.
We have a few different houses for our clients. 1 Mens house, 1 Womens house and 1 Co-ed.
