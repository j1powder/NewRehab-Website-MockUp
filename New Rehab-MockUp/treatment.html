Detox

Sometimes when a person is physically dependent on a substance, withdrawal effects can increase the addiction cravings. Sometimes withdrawal effects can be so severe that they cause physical illness and serious cases can even be fatal. We have medical detox facilities where you can separate yourself from the substance safely. Seeking treatment is hard enough already. Our state of the art Detox facility provides medical staff 24 hours a day.
